I am a Project Assistant Professor at the Department of Creative Informatics, The University of Tokyo. My research focuses on computational methods for representing, analyzing, and interacting with geometry and materials. I work across computer graphics and HCI to design algorithms and systems that connect digital models with the physical world and support creative interaction. I received a Ph.D. in Information Science and Technology from The University of Tokyo in 2023, advised by Takeo Igarashi, and have a background in architecture and art history.

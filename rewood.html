Re:Wood - Reconfigurable Furniture for Flexible Living

The above video shows the assembly and re-assembly of a 3D printed miniature model of our reconfigurable furniture system. With just 4 unique types of frame parts, it is possible to assemble 3 different pieces of furniture - a stool, a coffee table, and a shelving unit.

This project was supported by a collaborative research fund between Mercari Inc. R4D and RIISE, The University of Tokyo.
